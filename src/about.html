About GGIT

Growing Girls into Tech empowers young women aged 16-20 to become
future leaders in the dynamic world of technology. We nurture a passion for
tech through a structured mentorship program designed to equip you with
the skills and confidence to thrive. Regardless of your current experience
level, our program offers a tailored path to success.

Begin your journey in the Beginner Cohort, where interactive workshops
and hands-on learning lay the foundation for your tech knowledge. As you
progress, the Intermediate Cohort allows you to delve deeper into specific
tech areas with personalized guidance from experienced mentors. Finally,
the Expert Cohort empowers you to become a tech powerhouse by
collaborating with industry veterans on real-world projects and honing your
problem-solving skills.

Following your journey through the cohorts, you'll put your newfound
expertise to the test in a thrilling hackathon. This collaborative event allows
you to apply your skills, tackle real-world challenges, and network with
other aspiring tech enthusiasts. Your growth doesn't stop there – our
vibrant community fosters connections with peers and mentors, providing a
space for continuous learning and collaboration.

Join Growing Girls into Tech and unlock your potential to become a force for
the future. Let's shape the tech landscape together!
